Summary of Analysis

We analyzed scatterplots of variable pairs with multimodal pattern, aiming to detect switching points or shifts in causal direction.

We applied two simple, non-parametric techniques: binning and averaging, which divides the X-axis into equal-width segments to highlight nonlinear fluctuations and LOESS smoothing (Locally Estimated Scatterplot Smoothing), which fits local regression curves to detect changes in slope, indicating possible switches (frac = 0.1–0.4, tested over 10 iterations).
